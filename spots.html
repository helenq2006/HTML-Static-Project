Spots in NYC

Favorite Parts Least Favorite Parts
One of my favorite things about living in NYC is how diverse it is. You can try a bunch of different food without having to go to different countries. One of my least favorite things about living here is how it's a tourist city. Manhattan is always crowded with slow walkers trying to take pictures of Times Square
I also like how there are always things to do, whether it be events, popups, or a restaurant to eat at. It's hard to be bored. Everything here is also really expensive, especially in Manhattan, so I guess I don't like how cost of living is so high here.
