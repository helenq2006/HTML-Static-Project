Spots in NYC

Favorite parts

One of my favorite things about living in NYC is how diverse it is. You can try a bunch of different food without having to go to different countries.

I also like how there are always things to do, whether it be events, popups, or a restaurant to eat at. It's hard to be bored.

Least favorite parts

One of my least favorite things about living here is how it's a tourist city. Manhattan is always crowded with slow walkers trying to take pictures of Times Square and the Empire State Building.

Another thing I don't like is how bad the public transportation is. Unlike other developed cities in the world, our subway system is really nasty and unsafe.

Everything here is also really expensive, especially in Manhattan, so I guess I don't like how cost of living is so high here.
